About Palghar

Discover the rich history, vibrant culture, and natural beauty of Maharashtra's coastal paradise

Rich History & Heritage

Palghar, a district carved out of Thane in 2014, has a rich historical legacy dating back to ancient times. The region has been witness to various dynasties and rulers, including the Satavahanas, Western Chalukyas, and Marathas.

The district's strategic coastal location made it an important part of maritime trade routes, leading to the construction of numerous forts along its coastline. These forts, built by the Marathas and Portuguese, stand testament to the region's historical significance.

The name 'Palghar' itself has historical significance, derived from the words 'Pal' meaning protection and 'Ghar' meaning home, referring to its role as a protective shelter for seafarers and traders.
