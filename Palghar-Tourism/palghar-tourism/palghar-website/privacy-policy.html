Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Palghar Tourism, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)

  • Website usage data
  • Cookies and similar technologies

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your bookings and inquiries
  • Send you important updates about your bookings
  • Improve our services and website experience

  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information

  • Lodge a complaint with relevant authorities

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
