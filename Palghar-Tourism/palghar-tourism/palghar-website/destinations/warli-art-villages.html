Warli Art Villages

Experience the living tradition of tribal art and culture

About Warli Villages

The Warli villages of Palghar are home to one of India's oldest tribal art forms. These villages preserve the authentic traditions, lifestyle, and artistic heritage of the Warli tribe, known for their distinctive geometric art style.

Visitors can experience traditional village life, witness artists at work, and even participate in art workshops to learn the basics of Warli painting.

Cultural Highlights

Traditional Art

Watch skilled artists create intricate Warli paintings using traditional techniques and materials.

Village Life

Experience authentic tribal lifestyle, customs, and daily activities.

Art Workshops

Learn the basics of Warli art through hands-on workshops with local artists.
