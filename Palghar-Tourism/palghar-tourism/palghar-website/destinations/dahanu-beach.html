About Dahanu Beach

Dahanu Beach, located in the Palghar district of Maharashtra, is a pristine coastal destination known for its clean waters and peaceful atmosphere. The beach stretches for several kilometers, offering visitors plenty of space to relax and enjoy the natural beauty.

What makes Dahanu unique is its surrounding Chikoo (Sapodilla) orchards, which have earned it the nickname "Chikoo Bowl of Maharashtra". The combination of beach tourism and agricultural heritage creates a distinctive experience for visitors.

The beach is relatively less crowded compared to other beaches near Mumbai, making it perfect for those seeking a peaceful getaway from the city's hustle and bustle.

How to Reach

By Train

Dahanu Road railway station is well-connected to Mumbai via Western Railway. Regular local trains and express trains are available.

Distance from station to beach: 3 km

By Road

Take NH48 from Mumbai towards Gujarat. The journey takes approximately 3 hours by car. Regular ST buses also available from Mumbai.

Distance from Mumbai: 140 km

Local Transport

Auto-rickshaws and taxis are readily available from Dahanu Road station to the beach. You can also rent bikes for local sightseeing.

Best Time to Visit

Winter (Oct-Feb)

Best season to visit. Pleasant weather with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 25°C. Perfect for beach activities and sightseeing.

Summer (Mar-May)

Hot and humid. Temperatures can reach up to 35°C. Early morning or evening visits recommended.

Monsoon (Jun-Sep)

Heavy rainfall. Beach activities restricted. Beautiful green landscapes but not ideal for tourism.

Peak Season

December-February sees maximum tourists. Book accommodations in advance during weekends and holidays.

Tips & Guidelines

Do's

  • Carry sunscreen and beach essentials
  • Visit during early morning or sunset
  • Try local seafood specialties
  • Respect local customs and culture
  • Carry cash as cards might not work everywhere

Don'ts

  • Don't litter on the beach
  • Avoid swimming during high tide
  • Don't venture deep into the sea
  • Don't disturb marine life
  • Avoid beach activities during monsoon
