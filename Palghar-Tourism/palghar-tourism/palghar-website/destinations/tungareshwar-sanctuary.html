Tungareshwar Wildlife Sanctuary

A haven for wildlife and nature enthusiasts

About the Sanctuary

Tungareshwar Wildlife Sanctuary is a protected area spanning across 85.70 square kilometers in the Western Ghats. The sanctuary is known for its rich biodiversity, including diverse flora and fauna, and the sacred Tungareshwar Temple.

The sanctuary offers excellent opportunities for wildlife spotting, bird watching, nature photography, and trekking. Its varied landscape includes dense forests, streams, and hills, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers.

Wildlife & Nature

Flora

Dense forests with various tree species, medicinal plants, and rare orchids.

Fauna

Home to leopards, wild boars, various species of deer, and numerous bird species.

Bird Life

Paradise for bird watchers with over 200 species of birds.

Things to Do

  • Trekking and nature walks
  • Bird watching
  • Wildlife photography
  • Visit Tungareshwar Temple

Visitor Information

  • Timings: 6 AM - 6 PM
  • Best Time: October - March
  • Entry Fee: Nominal
  • Camera Fee: Applicable

How to Reach

  • By Train: Nearest station - Vasai Road
  • By Road: Well-connected to Mumbai
  • Distance: 45 km from Mumbai

Essential Tips

  • Wear comfortable hiking shoes
  • Carry water and snacks
  • Take guide for treks
  • Follow sanctuary rules
  • Avoid monsoon visits
