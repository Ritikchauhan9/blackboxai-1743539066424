Bordi Beach

Where golden sands meet Chikoo orchards

About Bordi Beach

Bordi Beach is a unique coastal destination that combines the charm of a pristine beach with the agricultural heritage of Chikoo farming. Known for its clean shoreline and peaceful atmosphere, Bordi offers visitors a perfect blend of nature and rural life.

The area is famous for its extensive Chikoo (Sapodilla) orchards, making it one of the largest Chikoo-producing regions in India. The combination of beach tourism and agricultural tourism makes Bordi a distinctive destination in Palghar.

Key Attractions

Chikoo Orchards

Visit the famous Chikoo farms and learn about the cultivation of this sweet fruit. Many orchards offer guided tours and fresh fruit tasting experiences.

Beach Activities

Enjoy the 17km long shoreline perfect for morning walks, sunbathing, and watching spectacular sunsets.

Rural Tourism

Experience local village life, traditional farming practices, and authentic rural Maharashtra culture.

Things to Do

  • Visit Chikoo orchards and farms
  • Beach activities and sunset viewing
  • Photography tours
  • Try local cuisine and Chikoo products
  • Stay in traditional farmhouses

Quick Information

  • Best Time: October to March
  • Location: Northern Palghar
  • Access: Road, Railway
  • Languages: Marathi, Hindi

How to Reach

By Train:

Nearest station: Bordi (Western Railway)

By Road:

Regular buses from Mumbai and Palghar

By Car:

3-hour drive from Mumbai via NH48

Weather

Winter: 16°C - 30°C
