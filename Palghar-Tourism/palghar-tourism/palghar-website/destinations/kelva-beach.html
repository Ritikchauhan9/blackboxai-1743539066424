Kelva Beach

A pristine coastal paradise with historic charm

About Kelva Beach

Kelva Beach is one of Palghar's most beautiful coastal destinations, known for its pristine shoreline, historic fort, and peaceful atmosphere. The beach stretches for several kilometers, offering visitors a perfect escape from urban life.

The beach is famous for its golden sands, clear waters, and the historic Kelva Fort that adds a cultural dimension to this natural paradise. The area is also known for its fishing community and local seafood delicacies.

Key Attractions

Kelva Fort

A 16th-century Portuguese fort offering panoramic views of the Arabian Sea and surrounding landscape. The fort's architecture and historical significance make it a must-visit attraction.

Sunset Point

Experience breathtaking sunsets from the beach's western end, where the sun paints the sky in vibrant colors as it dips into the Arabian Sea.

Local Markets

Explore the nearby fish market and local shops selling fresh seafood, traditional snacks, and handcrafted items.

Things to Do

  • Beach activities like swimming and sunbathing
  • Explore Kelva Fort and its surroundings
  • Photography at sunset point
  • Try local seafood delicacies
  • Shop at local markets

Quick Information

  • Best Time: October to March
  • Location: 15km from Palghar
  • Access: Road, Railway
  • Languages: Marathi, Hindi

How to Reach

By Train:

Nearest station: Kelva Road (2km)

By Road:

Regular buses from Palghar and Mumbai

By Car:

2-hour drive from Mumbai via NH48
