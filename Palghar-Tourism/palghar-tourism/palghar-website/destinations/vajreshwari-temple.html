Vajreshwari Temple

A sacred shrine with healing hot springs

About Vajreshwari Temple

Vajreshwari Temple is an ancient shrine dedicated to Goddess Vajreshwari, built on a hillock surrounded by hot springs. The temple is known for its spiritual significance and the healing properties of its natural hot springs.

The temple complex includes several smaller shrines and hot water kunds (pools) that are believed to have medicinal properties. The peaceful atmosphere and scenic location make it a popular destination for both pilgrims and tourists.

Temple Highlights

Main Temple

Ancient architecture and spiritual ambiance of the main shrine.

Hot Springs

Natural hot water springs with therapeutic properties.

Sacred Pools

Different kunds for ritual bathing and healing purposes.

Things to Do

  • Attend morning and evening aartis
  • Take a dip in the hot springs
  • Photography (exterior only)
  • Shop for religious items

Temple Information

  • Timings: 5 AM - 9 PM
  • Best Time: Year-round
  • Photography: Restricted
  • Footwear: Not allowed inside

How to Reach

  • By Bus: Regular buses from Palghar
  • By Train: Nearest station - Virar
  • By Car: Well-connected by road

Visitor Tips

  • Dress modestly
  • Carry towel for hot springs
  • Visit early morning
  • Respect temple customs
