Jawhar Hill Station

A serene retreat in the Sahyadri mountains

About Jawhar

Jawhar, often called the 'Mahabaleshwar of Palghar', is a picturesque hill station known for its pleasant climate, tribal culture, and natural beauty. Located in the Sahyadri ranges, it offers a perfect escape from urban life.

Key Attractions

Jai Vilas Palace

Historic palace showcasing royal architecture and tribal artifacts.

Dabdaba Falls

Magnificent waterfall offering stunning views and trekking opportunities.

Tribal Museum

Showcases rich cultural heritage of local Warli tribes.

Quick Information

  • Elevation: 1,500 ft
  • Climate: 15°C - 32°C
  • Best Time: October - March
  • Distance: 80km from Mumbai

How to Reach

  • By Bus: Regular ST buses from Mumbai
  • By Train: Nearest station - Dahanu Road
  • By Car: 3-hour drive from Mumbai
